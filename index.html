About Us

Empowering Through Inclusive Education

Datt niwasi matimand vidyalay atnur is dedicated to empowering visually impaired students through quality education, fostering independence, and nurturing their unique talents. Located in the heart of nilanga, Maharashtra, we provide a safe, supportive, and enriching environment where every child can thrive. Our school serves students from Grades 1 through 4, offering a comprehensive curriculum adapted to meet the specific needs of mentally challenged learners. With dedicated teachers, modern facilities, and 24-hour residential care, we ensure that every student receives the attention and support they deserve.

Vision • Values • Community

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and excellence in special education, where every child discovers their potential and builds a confident future.

Our Values

Compassion, excellence, inclusivity, and empowerment guide everything we do. We believe in nurturing both academic and personal growth.

Our Community

Students, dedicated staff, and supportive families come together to create a warm, caring environment where everyone belongs.
